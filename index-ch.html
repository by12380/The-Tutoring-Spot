What Makes Us Different?

We don't believe that doing well in college entrance exams requires purchasing expensive packages. In fact, students who get perfect scores study using the best materials that already exist in bookstores and libraries. However, sometimes students get stuck on problems because explanations provided in the books are static. They can only provide one way of explaining things. This is where we come in.

Using what we know the best resources are, our exceptional mentors provide students explanations from multiple angles as well as filling in the gaps, along with shortcuts, cross-referneces, and extensions to all kinds of practice problems. Our experiences help students avoid common mistakes, adopt useful strategies, saving them time to reinvent their own wheel.

With a reasonable price, students get the best out of our sessions. We know what an ideal prep class should be like because we have come from the same path, and we want to offer only what truly worked for us. We do this because we care!
