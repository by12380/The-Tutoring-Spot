Geometry

High school geometry is a major subject tested in college extrance exams such as SAT and ACT, especially in ACT. Although this subject introduces many rules related to lines, circles, and triangles, etc., repeatition is the effortless way to learn; therefore, practice is key. Having a good foundation in high school geometry is not only important to do well in college extrance exams, but also in advanced mathematics in college.

High school geometry introduces logic and proof, and students get first-time hands-on practice on how to solve problems using proof techniques. Many students find it hard to learn and give up half-way, but the truth is it's not and it doesn't have to be that way. With our teaching experience, we are masters at simplying and explaining all new concepts, and learning geometry is a breeze. This class will not only follow materials taught at school, but also quality problems collected over our teaching years, with an extra goal to prepare them for SAT and/or ACT.

In this class, bi-weekly quizzes are given, and based on their quizzes we hand-pick problems for each student to target each individual's need.

Algebra 2

High school algebra 2 is a major subject tested in college extrance exams such as SAT, ACT, and SAT Math II-C. In the redesigned SAT, the ability to formulate equation, from linear to quadratic to exponential models, are all fair game. In ACT, each topic from algebra 2 is equally tested, along with some topics that have been recently removed from the high school curriculum.

Besides for exam preperation, cleverly crafted algebra problems are the best pratices for critical thinking and problem solving. With our curated collection of problems, we have the best materials to help foster these critical thinking skills.

Thus, each topic in algebra 2 is essential and cannot be dismissed. Having strong foundation in algebra 2 is the key to do well in all upcoming mathematical challenges. This class will not only follow materials taught at school, but also those neglected yet necessary, with the goal to ready them for the SAT and ACT exams.

In this class, bi-weekly quizzes are given, and based on their quizzes we hand-pick problems for each student to target each individual's need.

SAT

In the redesigned SAT, not only do they ask students how to solve equations, but also how to formulate equations for data modeling. This overlaps with real-life use of statistics, where prediction and interpretation of data is commonplace in the technical field. Most student learn how to solve equations, but not formualte one, especially when it comes to doing word problems. We believe starting early is key, and with our carefully selected pratice problems and exams, we will ensure students master the real SAT.

Many of the topics in SAT math lies in the subject of algebra 2, and we make sure to revisit and reinforce these topics with our students.

In this class, weekly practice exams are given, and based on their exam performance we hand-pick problems as thier homework from past related Geometry and Algebra 2 topics for each student to refresh and reinforce their problem solving skills.
